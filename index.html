Hello I'm Dani van der Mijl

I'm a Front-End developer from The Netherlands

About Me

Hello, I'm Dani van der Mijl, born in Alkmaar which is a city in the Netherlands. I've always lived there with my family so far.

I am a software developer mainly interested in coding langueges for websites. Such as HTML, CSS, JS, SASS and more.

Get to known me!

I am borned and Raised in Alkmaar. I started at a school as a kid named Het kraaiennest. After that I went to Het Kompas. Just basic years nothing special happend until I went to het Jan Arentz. I did a Kader/TL study and realised I dont like to learn from Books etc. So I accepted that and went on with Kader. I went to the next school named the Van der Mei College. This is where I started working on Projects where you needed alot of Techical skills like editing videos, editing pictures, making animation etc.

I liked it alot and needed to decide which study I wantdd to follow so I could make it my job later on. I knew I wanted something that required techinal hands because I realised im pretty handy when it comes to that. I found the Study Software Developer on the Media College in Amsterdam and applied for it. After some talks and tests I got accepted and went to school.

I got introduced to Coding and started liking building websites. I kept enjoying it so I decided I wanted to be a Front-End Developer. I am now in my second, almost third year of this study.
